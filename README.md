# ShortSum
An app that makes studying easier and faster by summarzing content with AI.

## Image Summarizer
You can easily scan an image of your textbook or the content you're studying, and ShortSum will automatically extract the text using OCR and generate a summary with AI.

## Ask Questions
Easily ask questions with AI.

## Bad Handwriting Detector
If the content you're studying has bad handwriting or is hard to read, just scan it in ShortSum and ShortSum will try its best to extract the text from the content and will summarize it for you.

## Other Features
### Recheck
Retest your knowledge by typing in the original question and your answer, and AI will check it for you!

### Find Prime/Non-Prime
A math feature that you can use to easily find prime and non-prime numbers.

### Check Prime and Find HCF/LCM
Check if a number is prime or not and find the HCF or LMC of numbers.

### Article Summarizer
Quickly summarize an entire website with AI.

### Image Search
Search for images with Google.

## Why choose ShortSum?
### Completely free and open source
ShortSum is completely free to use and open source.

### Privacy first, your data is safe
ShortSum does not collect any data, and API keys are stored locally.

### No Subscriptions: Use your own OpenAI API Key
ShortSum is designed to have no subscriptions! You can use your own OpenAI API Key.
